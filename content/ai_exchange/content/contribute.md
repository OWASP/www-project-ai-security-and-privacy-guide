  <div class="max-w-7xl mx-auto">
        <h2 class="text-3xl sm:text-4xl font-bold text-gray-900 mb-8 text-center">
            Contribution guidelines
        </h2>
       <div class="text-gray-700 font-roboto font-normal text-[20px] leading-[30px] text-left">
  <p class="mb-4">
    We value every contribution to our project, but it’s important to be aware of certain guidelines:
  </p>

  <p class="mb-4">
    <span class="font-semibold">Avoid Advertising:</span> The OWASP AI projects should not be a medium for promoting commercial tools, companies, or individuals. The focus<br> should be on free and open-source tools when discussing the implementation of techniques or tests. While commercial tools generally aren’t <br>included, they may be mentioned in specific, relevant instances.
  </p>

  <p class="mb-4">
    <span class="font-semibold">Refrain from Unnecessary Self-Promotion:</span>  If you’re referencing tools or articles you’re affiliated with, please disclose this relationship in your<br> pull request. This transparency helps us ensure that the content aligns with the overall objectives of the guide.

  </p>
 
  <p>
    If you’re unsure about anything, feel free to 
    <a href="/connect" class="text-gray-600 font-medium underline">reach out to us</a> with your questions.
  </p>
